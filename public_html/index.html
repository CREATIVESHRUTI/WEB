Hi, and Welcome to Slice Of Art!

Myself Shruti Chandra, I warmly welcome you all to are page.

I have keen intrest in art be it- sketching, painting, drawing, doodle etc. I thought of showcasing My talent to the world other than keeping this to myself, that's the reason why we started this blog to show people our talent of art and also to inspire them to keep drawings and expressing there feelings and emotions my means of art. Art for me is something which gives us mental peace and satifaction, a means to of smile and happiness, relaxation and comfort.
